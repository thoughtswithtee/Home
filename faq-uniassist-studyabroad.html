FAQs: Uni-Assist Application form; Free Tuition; Student Loan

Q: What document should I prepare for uploading into uni-assist

A: Different universities, different courses request for different documents. As a result, the first advice would be to look into the documents listed in the application requirements of the course you want to apply to. Additionally, you may see these videos:


Q: Please can I get the uni-assist application page A: When on the prospective university webpage, most likely, a link to uni-assist will be found. If this is not the case then an internet search on google, bing or any other search platform for uni-assist will help find the uni-assist webpage.

Q: Help for applying for free tuition in Germany A: Most of the universities in Germany offer free tuition. However, every semester (6 months), there is a re-enrollment charge called semester contribution that each student must pay to be enrolled for the first time or re-enrolled each semester. Apart from this semester contribution, there is no tuition fee. Although the semester contribution applies to about all universities in Germany, a few universities in Germany require a tuition fee payment. Most commonly, students within one of the German states. Again this constitutes only a few of the universities in Germany. Having established this, to find universities in Germany that offer free tuition, a search on the daad website using filters will show these universities. To further see how this can be done, please see thisvideo. The search process is step by step documented and shown as it is being done on the daad website.

Q: Is it possible to apply for financial loan while in South Africa A: Two recommendations here. A.Check the kfw website. This advice is premium because things are updated regularly and to get the most up to date information would be to visit the website directly. Simply do an internet search of kfw student loan with google or bing or your favorite search engine to get website address and visit the faq section. B.The next recommendation would be to send an email to the Studentenwerk of your university asking them exactly this question. They may not only answer this question but even give further advice. Also, you may send an email to the international office of your university.

Q: Help, I can’t put the name of the school I studied at. A: Usually, there is the option to “type in” the name of the university if your university name is not in the list. Alternatively, you may send an email to the admissions office. Many times on the admissions page, there is contact information to the admission office.

Q: After uploading the documents, must I courier my documents to uni-assist before my application will be processed. A: On many occasions, you have to send your documents to uni-assist. However, on this, a case-by-case basis may be better. In your application, is it required to forward your documents? If it is required then do forward it. To know if it is required, see the email you received confirming that your application is submitted, see the requirements for application, see the information on the webpage after submitting your application.
