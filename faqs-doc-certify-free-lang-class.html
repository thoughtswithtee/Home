FAQs Document Certification Verification Free Language Class


Q: How to certify document before posting to the University A: There are multiple ways to certify documents. There are multiple ways to verify documents.
A. A notary public. This is one of the easiest and at the same time most expensive ways to certify a document. They are more accessible than other methods.
B. A Lawyer with an affiliation to a notary public
C. law court. Some courts for example, a high court has a department that is responsible for certifying documents.
D. The embassy of countries to be visited. Foreign offices also in most cases certify documents. It usually requires booking an appointment. The possibility of a document being accepted by the country to which it is being dispatched is highest among all the methods.

Q: Is there a possibility to be admitted to a German school and take advantage of the free German course? A: Normally, Yes. Many students that have gotten admission and are now enrolled as students take advantage of the free German course that many universities in Germany offer. Good to know is that the free German course runs for six months for a single level. On the other hand, taking a paid intensive German course, a level can be completed within 4 to 6 weeks. As such, there is quite a huge difference.

The caveat is that if you are not admitted yet and the admission requires a German language certificate then this is normally not applicable as a free German course because to have access to this course, you must be enrolled as a student already or make necessary payments. In this case, you may have to get the German language certificate first and use it in processing your admission.

It is possible in some cases to be admitted conditionally. In a case like this one can get admitted with a condition to fulfill the language requirement. One then uses this conditional admission to get a visa, travel into Germany and take a private course to complete the language requirement. The free language class is still not accessible because the admission is only conditional and there is not yet an the possibility to be enrolled as a student.

Q: How to create an account to fill in registration form A: Normally, the options “register”, “create account” “don’t have an account, create account ” are all possibilities to be used to create an account. They can be found on the “how to apply” page in many cases or the “apply now” page.

Q: Are international students able to apply for KFW loan? A: Normally yes. However, for the most updated information, visit the FAQ section of the KFW website or send an enquiry email to the international office of your university.
