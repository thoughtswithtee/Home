Guest: You feel that… its love, its like… but we men, e get why

Guest: I’ve been in like… three

TWTsBlog: And in these three cases, you didn’t love them, it was not love that made you go into the relationship

Guest: Its not “emotions”. Love is a natural thing, every man chooses to love differently. You can have “feelings” for people but it does not necessarily lead to marriage. The kind of love that leads to marriage grows. Its not the foundation.

TWTsBlog: Your first relationship, why her? If you now start telling she was intelligent in class, I will tell you that there was more than one intelligent person in class, if your heart wasn’t involved, why her?

Guest: I’m not saying the heart is not involved, I’m saying love is not…

TWTsBlog: So, what name do you use to describe what you feel in your heart?

Guest: Partnership… (this possibly means connection), business, investment, its like buying stock

TWTsBlog: (leaves the conversation, say what? Buying what? you don’t say… mouth agape… recovers) No, this is in your head, this is what you think, this is what you want to get out of it but I’m talking about how your heart feels towards them.

Guest: If you say “emotions”, that’s not the only person you feel something for… let’s use the word like, I like this one because of these special features, characters or these special abilities different from these other ladies

TWTsBlog: Ladies gush over getting married whereas, guys don’t have this behavior towards marriage. Why don’t guys feel this way?

Guest: Even we men want to spend our lives with you but we don’t obsess over it, we don’t fee it too much.

TWTsBlog: Ladies like attention, however some guys can’t wrap themselves around being able to give attention to ladies they are with.

Guest: Sincerely, a man needs attention too but not on this aspect of… women, I don’t know how God created you guys, you guys are too special, the way you guys want this attention like … If I am talking to you now, you’ll say look at me, you are not listening, you are not…

TWTsBlog: Yes now, why will I be talking to you and you’ll be doing another thing?

Guest: Maybe I’m trying to get something, I’m actually listening

TWTsBlog: Please, look at me when I’m talking to you

Guest: If he says okay, let me just… You’ll say, where are you going? Ahn ahn… "una talk no dey" finish? Like… women talk doesn’t finish?

TWTsBlog: It use to finish, what do you mean by that?

Guest: It does not because everyday, what you see from morning to evening, you want to download it to the man and he must listen.

TWTsBlog: Not everything, No. Not everything. Sometimes, we also have things that we are doing, we don’t have time. Its who we like that we want to share this thing with. Do you see us telling everybody? It is you “gan gan” that we want to tell.

Guest: Okay, okay really? Maybe you people should be reducing it?

TWTsBlog: What do you mean by that? you should feel privileged that we want to share this thing with you. Guys out there, please, feel privileged that she wants to tell you about her day.

Guest: We are not tape recorders oo (both laugh). We don’t have some external hard disk to carry all this load, we also have issues.
Okay, we want to appreciate all the ladies, to my lady, I am privileged that you are telling me things. At the same time, we have things we think about, if we share it, “ It is a lot oo, you may not be able to handle it”.

TWTsBlog: You know if we didn’t talk, you’ll also ask us if everything is alright.

Guest: Yea, because when you are too quiet. When we say don’t talk, you can just talk small. (both laugh)

TWTsBlog: Naturally, I feel that when you like someone, you want to spend time with them, you want to share your life with them and this involves telling them about things that’s going on in your life.

Guest: Men are not natural talkers (not always true) and you cannot know everything (goes both ways). With men, he may not be open to share everything like a lady would (not always true). It sometimes takes time, he may tell things in bits, he may not just tell everything because he does not know if you can actually absorb it or not and a man needs to defend his woman.

If you really care about a lady, you will want to protect her from everything even if that thing will not cause much harm to her, you will still want to protect her.

TWTsBlog: Okay, so, I feel the balance here would be that you are aware that she wants to share her experiences with you and you are also aware that she’ll be happy if you shared your experiences with her. If you do care about her, you keep this in mind.

Guest: For me, I’m not this talking type, I can play, talk, hype but when it comes to talk, talk, talk, I may not want to be disrespectful and say that the talk is getting long. I like it when you just say A, B, C and end it.

TWTsBlog: Summarily, guys may not necessarily be in relationships simply because of emotions, they look more into alignment of goals and personalities. I believe this goes both for guys and ladies. However, likeness many times features on both sides. I hope you found this insightful. Please share with someone that needs to read it.
